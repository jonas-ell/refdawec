Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 21, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how refdawecus.tech ("we", "us", or "our") handles information when you use our application ("App"), which integrates with the TikTok platform to publish video content.

1. Information We Collect

Our App does not collect personal information from end users. The App operates server-side and interacts with the TikTok API solely to publish video content to an authorized TikTok account. We do not store, share, or sell any user data.

2. TikTok API Usage

We use the TikTok Content Posting API to programmatically upload and publish short-form video content. The only TikTok data we access is the publish status of uploaded videos. We do not access profile data, follower lists, messages, or any other user information.

3. Data Storage

OAuth tokens used to authorize TikTok API access are stored securely on our server and are never shared with third parties. Video files are generated locally and deleted after successful upload.

4. Third-Party Services

Our App uses the following third-party services for content generation:

  • ElevenLabs — AI voice generation
  • Pexels — stock video footage
  • Apify — web scraping for trend research

Each of these services has its own privacy policy. We do not share personal user data with any of them.

5. Cookies

Our website does not use tracking cookies. We may use session cookies for authentication on our internal tools.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

7. Contact
