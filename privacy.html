Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2025

1. Overview

refdawec ("the App") is a personal tool for managing and scheduling TikTok posts. This policy explains what data is collected and how it is used.

2. Data We Collect

The App collects only the data necessary to function: your TikTok user ID and access tokens obtained through TikTok's OAuth flow. No personal information is sold or shared with third parties.

3. How We Use Your Data

Your TikTok credentials are used solely to authenticate your account and perform actions you explicitly request, such as scheduling and posting content to your TikTok profile.

4. Data Storage

Access tokens are stored locally on your own device. The App does not operate a central server that stores your personal data.

5. Third-Party Services

The App uses the TikTok API. Your use of TikTok is subject to TikTok's own Privacy Policy, available at tiktok.com.

6. Data Deletion

You can revoke the App's access to your TikTok account at any time through TikTok's app permissions settings. This will delete all stored access tokens.

7. Contact

For any privacy-related questions, please contact the App developer directly.
